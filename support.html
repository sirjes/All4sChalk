How to Play All Fours

All Fours is a trick-taking card game where points are scored for:

  • High: Highest trump in play (1 point)
  • Low: Lowest trump in play (1 point)
  • Jack: Jack of trumps (1 point)
  • Game: Most card points (1 or 2 points)
  • Hang: Winning all points in one hand (3 points)
  • Kick Points:
    • Turning up Jack (3 points)
    • Turning up Six (2 points)
    • Turning up Ace (1 point)

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track points?

Use the scoring buttons for each team:

  • Regular points: High, Low, Jack, Game (1 point each)
  • Special points: Hang (3 points)
  • Kick points: Use the ⚡ buttons for turned up cards
  • Subtract points: Use the -1 button if needed

What are the ⚡ (Kick) buttons for?

These buttons are used when a player turns up specific cards:

  • ⚡J - Turning up Jack (3 points)
  • ⚡6 - Turning up Six (2 points)
  • ⚡A - Turning up Ace (1 point)

Can I customize team names?

Yes! Simply tap on the team name to edit it. Your custom team names will be saved for future games.

How do I undo a move?

Use the "Undo" button in the game controls to reverse your last move. The app maintains a full history of all scoring actions.

What is "Game Worth 2"?

This option allows you to toggle between regular scoring (1 point) and double points (2 points) for game points. Enable this if your group plays with game points worth 2.

How do I view game history?

Tap the "History" button to view a chronological list of all scoring actions in the current game, including the running score after each move.

How do I share game results?

Use the share button (square with arrow) to capture the current scoreboard and share it via any available sharing method on your device.
